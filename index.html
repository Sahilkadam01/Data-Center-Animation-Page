Advanced Packaging

Packaging is no longer an afterthought because meeting the performance and power-efficiency needs of today can't be achieved without it. The silicon-packaging co-design is a fundamental part of the modern system canvas.

Advanced Packaging 2.5D

2.5D packaging enables multiple chiplets to communicate through a high-bandwidth silicon interposer. This approach improves performance, scalability and power efficiency while reducing latency.

Advanced Packaging 3.5D

3.5D integration combines advanced stacking technologies with high-density interconnects, enabling higher compute density and faster data movement across heterogeneous silicon components.
